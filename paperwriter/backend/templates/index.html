1. Introduction

Welcome to PaperWriter. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in strict accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act of India.

2. Data Collection and Purpose

We collect only the minimum necessary data required to provide our scholarly formatting services. This includes basic account information (email address) and the documents you explicitly save to our cloud servers. We do not use your documents to train artificial intelligence models without your explicit consent.

3. User Consent

By using our services, you provide explicit consent for the processing of your personal data solely for the purpose of document syncing, rendering, and management. You may withdraw this consent at any time through your Profile Settings.

4. Right to be Forgotten

Under the DPDP Act, you have the absolute right to erasure. You can permanently delete your account and all associated documents instantaneously using the "Delete Account" feature in your Profile Settings.

5. Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.
